The Terrifying Ghost of the Abandoned Mansion

Legend has it that the ghost of the old mansion is still haunting the halls to this day. Its ghostly apparition has been seen by many grown men who dared to enter the abandoned mansion. The ghost is said to be an old man with a long white beard and glowing eyes, who carries a lantern and walks with a limp. If you ever come across this ghost, beware, for it is said that he will follow you home and haunt you for the rest of your days!
